Correct. We don't ask for a card to start your trial. You get the full Pro Growth feature set for 29 days — build your site, plug in your tools, capture leads. If it isn't paying for itself by day 29, walk away.

How fast can a non-technical owner actually launch? +

Most owners are live and capturing leads in under 20 minutes. The AI builder writes the copy, picks the layout, sets up forms and the chatbot. You tweak colors, plug in your calendar and phone — done.

Will the AI sound like my business — or like a chatbot? +

Trained on your website, FAQs, services and tone of voice. It checks your calendar, qualifies leads, books appointments, and captures deposits. Every transcript is editable, so it improves with every call.

Keep it. iMax plugs into any existing site via a snippet — chat widget, lead forms, CRM, AI phone all work on day one. The website builder is optional.

None. Month-to-month, cancel anytime in one click. No setup fees on Starter or Pro Growth. Done-For-You includes white-glove onboarding at no extra cost.
